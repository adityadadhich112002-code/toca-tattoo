The Process

Here's How It Works.

A premium transformation deserves a structured process.

01 In-Person Consultation

We analyse your existing tattoo, skin tone, and anatomy before a single line is drawn.

02 Strategic Design

A bespoke layout is engineered around your muscle contours and cover-up requirements.

03 3D Concept Dev

Digital 3D modelling maps depth, flow and balance across your body before any ink touches skin.

04 Realistic Shading

High-contrast black & grey gradients are applied with cinematic precision, layer by layer.

05 Multi-Layer Technique

Multiple sessions of refined shading build true photorealistic depth that ages beautifully.

06 Long-Term Aesthetic

Aftercare guidance and touch-up support ensure your transformation lasts a lifetime.
